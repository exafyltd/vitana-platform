# Intelligent Matchmaking System

> A longevity-aligned matching engine that connects users with people, groups, events, services, products, locations, and live rooms -- grounded in health science rather than surface-level social signals.

## Core Principle

Maxina's matchmaking is fundamentally different from conventional social platforms. Instead of matching on appearance, demographics, or entertainment preferences, it matches on **longevity alignment** -- how well a connection supports both parties' healthspan, vitality, and long-term wellbeing. The scientific basis: health behaviors spread through social networks via proximity, repetition, and normalization. The people closest to you shape your habits, stress levels, motivation, and ultimately your lifespan.

## Match Score: 0 to 100

Every match carries a score measuring longevity alignment (not compatibility in a romantic sense). The score draws on:

- **Health goal alignment** -- Shared pillars of focus, similar improvement targets
- **Complementary strengths** -- One person strong where the other is growing (mutual exchange)
- **Shared habits and routines** -- Daily rhythms that create natural touchpoints
- **Geographic proximity** -- In-person connection carries additional longevity benefits
- **Professional connections** -- Relevant expertise (nutritionists, coaches, practitioners)
- **Lifestyle compatibility** -- Values, communication style, engagement level, relationship preference
- **Community overlap** -- Shared groups, events, related community activity

Scores are dynamic -- they shift as both parties evolve. A moderate match today may become highly relevant next month.

## Seven Match Types

| Type | Description | Longevity Role |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Person** | Individual connections -- peers, mentors, challenge partners, local connections | Deepest social bond potential; accountability and mutual support |
| **Group** | Communities organized around wellness topics, locations, or professional networks | Belonging and consistent social touchpoints (modern Moai) |
| **Event** | Workshops, fitness sessions, seminars, meetups, challenges | Turns intention into action; activates social and neurological systems |
| **Service** | Health professionals (nutritionists, coaches, sleep specialists, therapists) | Expert guidance for targeted progress; catches problems early |
| **Product** | Longevity-aligned supplements, wellness tools, recovery devices | Fills specific pillar gaps identified by health data |
| **Location** | Parks, gyms, wellness centers, healthy restaurants, trails | Environment supports habit building; enables in-person encounters |
| **Live Room** | Real-time hosted sessions (discussions, fitness, meditation, social) | Combines social connection with active engagement in real time |

## Daily Matches

Each day, the system delivers a curated set of matches to the user's recommendation inbox (also surfaced on the [[home-dashboard|Home Dashboard]] Matches tab). These are not a flood of profiles -- they are thoughtfully selected, each with a "Why this match" explanation.

Person match subtypes include:
- **Longevity peers** -- Similar journey stage, mutual support
- **Mentors and guides** -- Ahead in an area where the user is growing
- **Challenge partners** -- Thrive on shared goals and friendly motivation
- **Local connections** -- Open to in-person meetups
- **Professional resources** -- Relevant health expertise

Match types rotate daily for diversity.

## "Why This Match" Explanations

Every match includes a transparent explanation connecting the user's profile/goals to specific attributes of the match. Examples:

- Person: "You both prioritize morning exercise and share a focus on improving sleep quality. They have been working on a consistent evening wind-down routine for three months."
- Group: "This group focuses on anti-inflammatory nutrition, which aligns with your current health goal."
- Event: "This workshop on stress management aligns with your mental health focus. Two members of your network are attending."
- Service: "Based on your sleep data trends, a consultation with a sleep specialist could identify patterns self-tracking may not reveal."

Explanations never reveal private health data -- they describe alignment in general terms (shared direction, not specific metrics). The other party receives equally general explanations about the user.

## How Matches Improve Over Time

The system learns continuously from user behavior:

- **Accept** -- Reinforces the factors that drew the user in
- **Dismiss** -- Reduces weight of undesired match characteristics
- **Engage deeply** -- Strong engagement signals strengthen those alignment factors
- **Connection fades** -- Informs what genuine longevity alignment looks like for this specific user

## Longevity-Based Social Clustering

Beyond individual matches, the system surfaces dynamic micro-communities (clusters) organized around specific health focuses, lifestyle approaches, or longevity goals -- e.g., cold exposure and metabolic health, cognitive longevity, parents balancing family and fitness. Clusters are organic and shaped by actual behavior.

## User Preferences and Privacy

- Users set explicit preferences: geographic range, age range, health focus areas, match types
- Health data is never shared with matches
- Users can dismiss, block, or simply not respond without pressure
- Match explanations use general alignment terms, never specific health metrics or Vitana Index scores

## The Moai Concept

Inspired by Okinawan tradition, Maxina supports the formation of **moai** -- small, committed circles of members who support each other's longevity journeys long-term. Moai members check in regularly, share health data voluntarily, hold each other accountable through genuine care. The system helps form moai based on compatibility (shared values, complementary strengths, similar life stages, aligned priorities).
