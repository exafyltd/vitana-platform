# Discover Feature and Marketplace

> A longevity-curated marketplace of supplements, wellness products, health professionals, and deals -- personalized to each user's Vitana Index, pillar gaps, and health goals.

## Overview

The Discover section is Vitanaland's marketplace, but it operates under a strict longevity lens: every product, service, and professional listed must support at least one health pillar or longevity dimension. It is not a generic health store. It is a curated, personalized resource where what users see is shaped by their [[vitana-index|Vitana Index]], pillar data, goals, activity, and past interactions.

## Key Sections

### AI Picks for You
The most personalized part of Discover. The Autopilot selects products, services, and resources based on:
- Vitana Index and pillar sub-scores (surfaces items addressing weakest pillars)
- Stated health goals
- Recent activity and trends (e.g., improving exercise momentum or declining mental health)
- Past interactions (views, saves, purchases, dismissals create a learning loop)
- Community signals (what works for members with similar profiles)

Every AI Pick includes an explanation: "Your sleep quality has averaged below your 30-day baseline for two weeks. This magnesium glycinate supplement has been shown to support sleep quality."

User actions on picks: Explore, Save for later, Dismiss (refines future picks), Purchase/Book.

### Supplements and Wellness Products
Curated catalog organized by category:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **Vitamins and Minerals** | Vitamin D, B vitamins, magnesium, iron, zinc, calcium |
| **Adaptogens** | Ashwagandha, rhodiola, lion's mane |
| **Protein and Amino Acids** | Whey, plant-based, collagen, amino acid blends |
| **Hydration and Electrolytes** | Electrolyte supplements, mineral-enhanced products |
| **Sleep Support** | Melatonin, magnesium glycinate, valerian, chamomile |
| **Cognitive Support** | Omega-3, phosphatidylserine, bacopa, nootropic blends |
| **Recovery and Anti-Inflammation** | Omega-3s, curcumin, tart cherry extract, foam rollers, massage tools |

Products are vetted for evidence alignment, quality/sourcing, longevity relevance, and community feedback. Each item is tagged with the health pillars it supports.

Important principle: "Supplements complement a healthy lifestyle. They do not replace it." The ORB will redirect users toward habit changes when that is the more appropriate solution.

### Doctors and Coaches
Health professionals within Vitanaland: nutritionists, fitness coaches, sleep specialists, therapists, preventive medicine doctors, wellness consultants. Users can:
- Browse by specialty
- See credentials and community ratings
- Book consultations directly

Service matches are driven by health data and pillar gaps -- not random browsing.

### Deals and Special Offers
Time-limited offers and member-exclusive pricing from vetted wellness partners. Every deal must support longevity principles.

### Category Browsing
Browse by pillar-aligned categories: Nutrition, Fitness, Sleep, Mental Health, Hydration, Recovery, Cognitive Health. Or let the ORB guide via conversation ("What should I be looking at in Discover right now?").

## The Longevity Lens

The curation standard for Discover listings:
- Must support at least one health pillar or longevity dimension
- Evidence alignment (published research, established science, documented traditional use)
- Quality and sourcing (transparent sourcing, third-party testing, GMP)
- Longevity relevance (clear connection to long-term health)
- Community feedback (ongoing quality signal from reviews)

Products making unsubstantiated claims are excluded.

## Community Reviews
Every product includes reviews from Vitanaland members on similar longevity journeys. Reviews are more contextually relevant than generic online reviews because reviewers share the health-focused context.

## Ordering and Checkout
Products can be purchased directly within Vitanaland. Cart, checkout, and order tracking are integrated. Payments connect to the [[financial-longevity|wallet system]] (credits, cash, or standard payment methods).

## Connection to the Longevity Economy

Discover is where the [[financial-longevity|longevity economy]] becomes tangible. Users can create curated wellness lists of products they trust and earn commissions when others purchase through their recommendations. The Autopilot amplifies recommendations by matching them to the right audience -- connecting trusted recommendations with people most likely to benefit.
