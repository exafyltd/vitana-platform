## What it is

Data & Consent controls what profile, activity, health-adjacent, marketplace, media, and monetization data Vitana may use or share.

Sharing controls campaigns, distribution, social posting, data consent, and monetization with privacy first.

## Why it matters

Privacy is first priority. Data can create value through better matches, campaigns, referrals, and opportunity discovery, but it must be permissioned, auditable, and reversible.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/sharing/data-consent`.

Sidebar path: **Sharing → Data & Consent**.

Module: **Sharing**.

## What you see on this screen

- Consent categories for profile, activity, health, marketplace, media, and business data
- Data monetization and Autopilot Referrals settings
- Audit history of shared or used data
- Export, revoke, pause, delete, and support actions

## How to use it

1. Explain the benefit and risk before enabling consent.
2. Enable only the exact categories the user accepts.
3. Keep sensitive health data private unless the user approves a specific use.
4. Show audit history when asked what was shared.
5. Revoke consent immediately when requested.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Show my privacy settings."
- "Turn on data monetization."
- "What data is being shared?"
- "Stop sharing my health data."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

Sharing controls campaigns, distribution, social posting, data consent, and monetization with privacy first. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
