## What it is

Campaign Detail is the Campaign Detail screen in the Sharing module. It gives the user a focused place to understand the current state, choose the next step, and ask Vitana for help.

Sharing controls campaigns, distribution, social posting, data consent, and monetization with privacy first.

## Why it matters

Sharing controls campaigns, distribution, social posting, data consent, and monetization with privacy first. It should help the user live better through the five longevity pillars while making useful community or income opportunities easier to act on.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/sharing/campaigns/:campaignId`.

Sidebar path: **Sharing → Campaign Detail**.

Module: **Sharing**.

## What you see on this screen

- Campaign, channel, audience, consent, or analytics cards
- Draft, schedule, connect, disconnect, activate, pause, or revoke actions
- Privacy, disclosure, and audit information
- Autopilot sharing suggestions

## How to use it

1. Confirm the goal, audience, channel, and timing.
2. Preview public content before sending.
3. Check Data & Consent before sharing personal or health-related information.
4. Review analytics and pause campaigns that no longer fit.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Create a campaign."
- "Connect my channels."
- "What data is shared?"
- "Stop sharing this."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

Sharing controls campaigns, distribution, social posting, data consent, and monetization with privacy first. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
