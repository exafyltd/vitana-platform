## What it is

Discover is the Vitana marketplace — the curated catalog of products and services matched to your health profile and your active Life Compass. The Overview screen is the front door: a personalised feed of supplement suggestions, wellness services, doctor and coach availability, current deals, and items related to recent activity. Everything is filtered through the Intent Engine, so the recommendations should reflect what would actually help you, not just what's for sale.

The marketplace is honest about why each item is shown — every recommendation card carries a short "why" line ("matches your low Hydration pillar", "reorder of last month's purchase", "discounted in your region this week").

## Why it matters

Most marketplace experiences either flood you with everything (catalogue browsing) or recommend by sales funnel (whatever the seller pays to surface). Discover is a third pattern: the recommendation is grounded in *your* data — your weakest pillar, your Compass direction, your purchase history, your trackers. The same store can feel useful or spammy depending on whether matches are real; Discover is built on the assumption that match honesty is the unlock.

It's also where the Vitana Wallet meets real-world value. Most listings are payable in fiat or credits, and earning rewards apply on first purchases through the marketplace.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/discover`. Sidebar: **Discover → Discover Overview**. The Cart icon in the header (chapter 4.9) is your in-flight purchases; the Orders sub-screen (chapter 4.6) is your purchase history.

## What you see on this screen

- **Header** "Discover" with a search button and a Cart shortcut showing item count
- **Suggested products carousel** — top picks ranked by your current health gaps
- **Scope selector** — filter recommendations by health pillar (Nutrition, Sleep, Movement, Mental, etc.) or by a Life Compass goal
- **Sub-category tiles** — Supplements, Wellness Services, Doctors & Coaches, Deals & Offers
- **Hidden by limitations footer** — small text noting which categories are filtered out by your privacy / consent settings
- **Master action popup** — bulk-add items to cart or to a wave
- **Affiliate disclosure badge** on every external listing
- **Recent orders strip** — quick reorder shortcuts

## How to use it

1. Open Discover. Scan the Suggested products carousel — the cards explain why each is shown.
2. To narrow recommendations: tap the scope selector and pick a pillar or Compass goal.
3. Tap any product card to open Product Detail (4.7) where you can read full specs, reviews, and pricing.
4. Tap **Add to cart** to queue items for purchase, or tap **Buy now** for single-item checkout.
5. To see all supplements / services / doctors in a category, tap the corresponding sub-category tile.
6. To find a current deal, open Deals & Offers (4.5) — promo windows, regional discounts, bundle pricing all live there.
