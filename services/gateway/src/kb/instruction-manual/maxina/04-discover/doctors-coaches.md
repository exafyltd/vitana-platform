## What it is

Doctors & Coaches is the Doctors & Coaches screen in the Discover module. It gives the user a focused place to understand the current state, choose the next step, and ask Vitana for help.

Discover is the marketplace layer for products, providers, services, offers, orders, and cart decisions matched to health and opportunity goals.

## Why it matters

Discover is the marketplace layer for products, providers, services, offers, orders, and cart decisions matched to health and opportunity goals. It should help the user live better through the five longevity pillars while making useful community or income opportunities easier to act on.

## What you see on this screen

- Marketplace cards with price, provider, availability, and relevance reason
- Filters for pillar, goal, category, price, and location
- Save, compare, add to cart, book, claim, refund, or checkout actions
- Disclosures, warnings, and support links

## How to use it

1. Search or filter by the goal the user names.
2. Open details before purchase or booking.
3. Check price, terms, warnings, and fit reason.
4. Ask for explicit confirmation before checkout, booking, refund, or subscription actions.
