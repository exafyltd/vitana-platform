## What it is

Maxina is the community you belong to. On Vitanaland (vitanaland.com) Vitana is multi-tenant — different communities run on the same Vitana platform but each with its own brand, its own group of members, its own events, its own knowledge. Maxina is one of those communities; if you signed up via the Maxina portal, this is the one you're in.

Your **role** in Maxina is "community" — that means you're a member, not a developer or platform admin. The Maxina admin curates groups, events, and the knowledge base for the community; you participate, post, attend, learn.

## Why it matters

The community is the social layer of Vitana. Everything the Vitana Index, the Autopilot, and ORB do is *personal* to you, but you're not doing it alone. Maxina is the surface where you find people on the same journey, attend events, follow creators, and join challenges. It is also the audience for anything you choose to share — a streak you've hit, a wave you've completed, a post about a recent diary insight.

Knowing your tenant matters when something looks wrong: if you see another community's branding, a feature you didn't expect, or events that aren't relevant, the most likely cause is that you signed in to a different tenant. You can always check current tenant under Settings → Tenant & Role.

## Where to find it

- **Maxina Portal Login (`/maxina`)** — your dedicated entry point
- **Settings → Tenant & Role (`/settings/tenant`)** — where you confirm or switch tenant context
- **Community → Community Overview (`/community`)** — the home of community life

## How to use it

1. Always sign in via `/maxina` (or click the Maxina-branded link from email). This makes sure your session is scoped to the Maxina tenant.
2. Open Settings → Tenant & Role to verify. You should see "Tenant: Maxina, Role: community."
3. To join community life: open Community → Community Overview. You'll see today's events, live rooms, and member highlights.
4. To attend an event, RSVP, or join a live room: tap the card and follow the in-screen flow. Most actions reward credits or tokens.
5. If something feels off (wrong branding, missing data), sign out and sign back in via `/maxina`. Cross-tenant sessions are the most common cause of "I don't see my stuff."
