## What it is

The Vitana Wallet holds three different kinds of value, and it's worth knowing the difference. **Fiat** is real money — euros, dollars — that you deposit to fund purchases. **Credits** are platform currency you spend on supplements, services, courses, and marketplace items inside Vitana; they do not fluctuate in price. **Tokens** are the staked-and-earned currency rewarded for activity (logging streaks, completing waves, referring users); tokens can be staked for higher reward rates or held. **Subscriptions** are recurring fiat charges for premium features (advanced labs, premium coaching slots).

**Rewards** is the activity-based earnings ledger — every time you complete a streak, hit a tier, or fulfil an Autopilot wave, the system credits a reward you can see on Wallet → Rewards.

## Why it matters

You need to know which kind of money you're spending before you tap Buy. Most marketplace items can be paid in either credits or fiat, and the price differs slightly. Subscription charges always pull from fiat. Tokens are forward-looking — a long-term staking position — not for everyday spending. Mixing them up costs you.

The Wallet also doubles as the **identity** for your Vitana economic life. Your Vitana ID is the address peer-to-peer payments are sent to (request from `@anna`, send to `@bob`), and the Wallet is the storage layer behind it.

## Where to find it

- **Wallet → Wallet Overview (`/wallet`)** — the snapshot view with balances and recent transactions
- **Wallet → Balance** — detailed credits, tokens, fiat breakdown
- **Wallet → Subscriptions** — your active recurring charges
- **Wallet → Rewards** — the rewards ledger and earnings forecast

The wallet header button (icon top-right) is a one-tap shortcut from any screen.

## How to use it

1. Open Wallet to see your three balance cards: Credits, Tokens, Fiat.
2. To add fiat: tap **Add Funds**, choose payment method, confirm. The amount appears in your fiat balance.
3. To buy credits with fiat: tap **Buy Credits**, enter amount, confirm. The system converts and updates both balances.
4. To stake tokens for higher reward rates: open **Stake** and choose a duration. Staked tokens are locked for the period.
5. To send to another user: tap **Send**, type their `@handle`, choose amount and currency, confirm.
6. To request from another user: tap **Request**, type their `@handle`, choose amount, send. They get a notification.
7. To see why you earned a reward, open Wallet → Rewards. Each entry has a "why" line ("3-day diary streak", "completed Wave 4").
