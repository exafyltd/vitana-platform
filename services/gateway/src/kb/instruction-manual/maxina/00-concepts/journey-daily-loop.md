## What it is

My Journey is the user's guided path through Vitanaland. It combines the Life Compass, Vitana Index, Daily Diary, Calendar, reminders, Autopilot actions, community events, and recommendations into a practical daily loop. The loop is: check awareness, choose the next action, do it, log or confirm it, and let Vitana adapt.

The Daily Diary is the fastest manual input for the five pillars and personal context. Calendar and Reminders hold time. Home Actions and Autopilot decide what should happen next. Daily Summary explains what changed.

## Why it matters

A longevity companion must become part of everyday life. The user should not need a perfect plan before starting. They need a small repeatable loop that makes health and opportunity visible: measure, act, reflect, adjust.

This is also how Vitana becomes self-improving. Each completed action, skipped task, diary note, event, match, or connected app signal helps the assistant tune future recommendations.

## Where to find it

Use Home for today, Home Actions for the next tasks, Daily Summary for the morning brief, Diary for logging, Calendar for timing, and AI Recommendations or Autopilot for a fuller queue. The Life Compass appears inside Journey-related surfaces and tunes the whole loop.

## How to use it

1. Start with Daily Summary or Home each morning.
2. Check the Vitana Index and weakest pillar.
3. Choose one action from Home Actions or Autopilot.
4. Complete the action or schedule it on Calendar.
5. Log health, mood, meals, water, sleep, exercise, or reflections in Diary.
6. At night, review what changed and let Vitana prepare tomorrow.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Open My Journey."
- "What should I do today?"
- "Open my Daily Diary."
- "Plan my day around my calendar."
- "Remind me to complete my health plan."
- "Summarize yesterday."
