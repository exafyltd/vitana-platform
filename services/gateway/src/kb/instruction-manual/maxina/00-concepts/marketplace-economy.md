## What it is

The Marketplace and Longevity Economy connect health improvement with income creation. Discover helps users find products, services, providers, and offers. My Business helps users create services or opportunities. Wallet tracks credits, rewards, subscriptions, and commissions. Sharing and Campaigns help distribute offers and media with consent.

Find a Match belongs to this economy too. It brings the right people together for accountability, events, partnerships, client relationships, and new businesses.

## Why it matters

Vitana is not only a personal wellbeing tool. The goal is also to help people create sustainable income in a longevity-focused economy. A user may start by improving sleep or hydration, then become a coach, creator, event host, reseller, partner, or service provider. The system should help them build, operate, and grow with minimal manual effort.

Autopilot should surface marketplace and business actions only when they are relevant and privacy-safe. It should explain why an opportunity fits and what confirmation is required.

## Where to find it

Use Discover for products and services, Community or Home Matches for people, My Business for offers and clients, Wallet for value, Sharing for distribution, and Data & Consent for privacy and monetization permission.

## How to use it

1. Identify the user's goal: improve health, find a provider, earn income, promote a service, or form a partnership.
2. Use Discover for buying or booking.
3. Use Find a Match for people and partnerships.
4. Use My Business to create an offer or service.
5. Use Sharing and Campaigns to distribute with consent.
6. Use Wallet and Rewards to track income and benefits.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Help me build a new business."
- "Find a client or partner."
- "Show marketplace tools for my goal."
- "Create a campaign for my service."
- "How can I earn income in Maxina?"
