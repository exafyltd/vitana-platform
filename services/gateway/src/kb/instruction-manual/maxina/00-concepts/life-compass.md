## What it is

The Life Compass is your active life goal — the single direction you point Vitana in for the next 90 days. You pick from a small set of life directions (build financial freedom, deepen relationships, restore health, grow career, expand education, deepen spirituality), and Vitana tunes everything it suggests so it actually moves that needle.

Behind the scenes the Compass acts as a lens: the same Vitana Index, the same Autopilot recommendations, the same morning brief, but ranked differently depending on what direction you chose. A Compass set to "restore health" makes the recommendation engine prioritise sleep and movement; a Compass set to "deepen relationships" surfaces community and reconnect actions. One goal, one direction, one Compass at a time.

## Why it matters

Without a Compass, Vitana is a generic wellness tool — it will cheer for any pillar that moves up. With a Compass, every nudge has a *why* you can recognise: "you said you wanted to deepen relationships, so today's first card is calling Anna." That is the difference between an app that tracks your life and a system that helps you build it.

The Compass is also how Vitana knows what *not* to do. Recommendations that don't move your active direction get dampened so the queue stays focused. You can swap the Compass anytime, and Vitana re-tunes within the next session.

## Where to find it

Three entry points, all opening the same Compass modal:

1. **Settings → Tenant & Role** — your active Compass shows under "Your Vitana setup"
2. **My Journey (Autopilot) overview** — the Compass card sits next to the recommended waves
3. **Health → Vitana Index Detail (`/health/vitana-index`)** — the Compass card sits below your score

Direct URL: there is no standalone page; the Compass lives as an overlay you can open from any of the three host screens above.

## How to use it

1. Open one of the host screens above and find the **Life Compass** card.
2. Tap it to open the picker. You'll see the available directions with a one-line description for each.
3. Choose the one that matches what you actually want for the next 90 days. Don't overthink it — you can change it.
4. Tap **Set as my Compass**. Vitana confirms and starts tuning. The next morning brief, the next Autopilot wave, the next Did You Know card will all reflect the new direction.
5. To swap, repeat. Vitana keeps the previous Compass in your timeline so you can see how your direction has evolved over time.
