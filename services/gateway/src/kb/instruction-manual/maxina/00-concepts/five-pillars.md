## What it is

The Five Pillars are the canonical dimensions Vitana measures: **Nutrition, Hydration, Exercise, Sleep, Mental**. Every action you log, every signal a tracker syncs, every insight a coach gives you maps to one of these five. Each pillar is scored 0-200; together they make up your Vitana Index.

You may have heard about a "social" or "environmental" pillar in earlier writing. Those are real life domains, but they roll into Mental — community time, meditation, focus, and the quality of your environment all show up as Mental score because they affect the same biological surface. Five pillars, not seven, by design.

## Why it matters

The pillars are how Vitana converts a vague "I want to be healthier" into something measurable. Each pillar has a baseline (from your survey), a logging surface (Diary, manual entry, tracker), and a set of interventions. When your score moves, you know which pillar moved it. When your Compass changes, the pillars get re-prioritised.

The pillars also stop you from playing favourites with yourself. The balance factor in the Vitana Index is what punishes a 200 Nutrition / 30 Sleep imbalance with a low total — Vitana is honest that you can't fix the whole picture by perfecting one pillar.

## Where to find it

- **Health → Pillars (`/health/pillars`)** — the dedicated pillar-by-pillar drill-down screen
- **Health → Health Overview** — pillar bars on the Vitana Index Mini card
- **Health → Vitana Index Detail (`/health/vitana-index`)** — full pillar bars + recent activity per pillar

Direct URL for the dedicated screen: `/health/pillars`.

## How to use it

1. Open the Pillars screen and read each bar. The label under each pillar tells you what's currently driving its score (e.g. "mostly baseline", "logged 3 of 7 days last week", "sleep tracker syncing").
2. Identify your weakest pillar — that's where the next intervention has the biggest payoff.
3. Tap the weak pillar to see what specific actions are available. Each pillar has its own "easiest action" in the Diary or via a connected tracker.
4. After logging, watch the score move. If it doesn't, the action probably belonged to a different pillar — Vitana will explain why next time you ask.
