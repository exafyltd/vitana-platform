## What it is

Matches shows people Vitana thinks are relevant for friendship, accountability, events, coaching, partnership, clients, or business collaboration.

Home turns awareness into action: context, actions, matches, and AI feed should reduce thinking and move the user toward one useful next step.

## Why it matters

Find a Match is both a wellbeing feature and an opportunity engine. The right person can improve mental health, support a habit, attend an event, become a client, or help create a business.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/home/matches`.

Sidebar path: **Home → Matches**.

Module: **Home**.

## What you see on this screen

- Daily match cards with compatibility reasons
- Shared interests, groups, events, location, and Life Compass signals
- Profile Preview, connect, message, save, and dismiss actions
- Filters for friend, partner, client, coach, event, or business intent

## How to use it

1. Ask what kind of match the user wants.
2. Read the reason before opening the profile.
3. Use Profile Preview before messaging.
4. Draft the first message and ask for confirmation before sending.
5. Use feedback on poor matches to improve future ranking.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Find me a match."
- "Who should I meet?"
- "Find a business partner."
- "Send this match a message."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant
