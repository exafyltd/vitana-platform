## What it is

Home is the landing screen — the first thing you see after sign-in and the surface you return to every morning. It is a curated daily feed: longevity-science news from a vetted set of sources, community updates from your Maxina tenant, the Did You Know card for the day, the Priority of Day banner, and a Welcome Back banner if you have been away. The feed is searchable and filterable, and a refresh button at the top pulls the freshest items.

Home also surfaces three quick-access chips on mobile — your **Vitana Index**, your **Autopilot** action count, and the **Calendar** button — so the most important system signals are one tap away without having to dig into another screen.

## Why it matters

Most users don't open Vitana with a specific goal in mind — they open it the way they open a news app: to check in. Home is designed for that moment. The cards are ranked so the highest-impact items rise first: a Priority of Day banner if Vitana has detected something urgent, then the Did You Know tour card if you are still in your first 30 days, then the news feed with longevity articles tagged to your current Compass and weakest pillar.

Home is also where the **AI Feed** lives as a sub-tab — a stream of insights specifically generated for you (rather than curated articles).

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/home`. Sidebar: **Home → Home Overview**. It is also the default landing route after sign-in. Sub-tabs (Context, Actions, Matches, AI Feed) sit under the same Home module.

## What you see on this screen

- **Standard header** with the Vitana logo, your handle, and the global utility buttons (Calendar, Notifications, Wallet, Autopilot, Search)
- **Mobile mode pill** (mobile only) — switches between the desktop and mobile-optimised layouts
- **Vitana Index chip and Autopilot chip** (mobile only) — your live score and pending action count, tappable shortcuts
- **Welcome Back banner** — appears if you have not opened Vitana for several days, showing a one-line catch-up
- **Priority of Day banner** — appears if Vitana has detected an urgent next-best-action; tap to act or dismiss
- **Did You Know card** — your daily 30-day-tour card with a "Show me" or "Tell me more" CTA
- **News feed** — longevity-science articles in chronological order, filterable by All / Longevity / Community
- **Search** — expandable search button at the top of the feed; searches title, summary, source, and tags
- **Refresh button** — pulls the latest news articles
- **Sub-navigation** for Context, Actions, Matches, AI Feed

## How to use it

1. Open Home each morning. The cards are ranked top-down by priority — read the first two before scrolling.
2. If a Priority of Day banner shows, act on it or dismiss; do not scroll past unprocessed.
3. Tap the Did You Know card and either say "Show me" (open the relevant feature), "Tell me more" (have ORB explain), or "Skip" (mark seen).
4. Browse the news feed casually; long-press an article to save it to Memory.
5. Use the filter tabs (All / Longevity / Community) to narrow the feed when you have a specific intent.
6. Tap the Vitana Index chip to jump straight to Health → Vitana Index Detail; tap the Autopilot chip to open the Autopilot popup.
