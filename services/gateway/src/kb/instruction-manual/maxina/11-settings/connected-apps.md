## What it is

Connected Apps manages wearables, calendars, music apps, social channels, health services, and other integrations connected to Vitana.

Settings gives the user control over identity, privacy, connected apps, notifications, billing, support, and tenant role.

## Why it matters

Connected data lets Vitana measure health with less manual effort and personalize guidance more accurately. It also powers music, media, calendar, sharing, and Autopilot workflows.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/settings/connected-apps`.

Sidebar path: **Settings → Connected Apps**.

Module: **Settings**.

## What you see on this screen

- Connected app list and sync status
- Wearable, calendar, music, health, and social connectors
- Connect, disconnect, refresh, and permission controls
- Data freshness warnings and consent notes

## How to use it

1. Choose the app type the user wants to connect.
2. Explain what data the app can read or write.
3. Connect only after the user grants permission.
4. Check sync freshness when data seems missing.
5. Disconnect apps the user no longer trusts.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Connect my wearable."
- "Connect my music app."
- "Sync my calendar."
- "Disconnect this app."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

Settings gives the user control over identity, privacy, connected apps, notifications, billing, support, and tenant role. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
