## What it is

Balance is the Balance screen in the Wallet module. It gives the user a focused place to understand the current state, choose the next step, and ask Vitana for help.

Wallet connects health participation and business participation to credits, benefits, rewards, subscriptions, and commissions.

## Why it matters

Wallet connects health participation and business participation to credits, benefits, rewards, subscriptions, and commissions. It should help the user live better through the five longevity pillars while making useful community or income opportunities easier to act on.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/wallet/balance`.

Sidebar path: **Wallet → Balance**.

Module: **Wallet**.

## What you see on this screen

- Balance, reward, subscription, or benefit state
- Pending versus available value
- Transaction, invoice, renewal, redeem, or payout controls
- Warnings before financial changes

## How to use it

1. Read available and pending values separately.
2. Explain why a reward or charge exists.
3. Use full history for disputes.
4. Require confirmation before redeeming, transferring, subscribing, cancelling, or paying.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Show my wallet."
- "What rewards did I earn?"
- "Cancel this subscription."
- "Can I use credits?"

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

Wallet connects health participation and business participation to credits, benefits, rewards, subscriptions, and commissions. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
