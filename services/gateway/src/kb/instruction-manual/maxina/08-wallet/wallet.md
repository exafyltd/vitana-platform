## What it is

Wallet Overview is the snapshot of every kind of value you hold in Vitana — fiat (real money), credits (platform spend currency), tokens (activity-earned reward currency), active subscriptions, and the most recent transaction history. It is the home screen of the Wallet module and the first place you go when you want to understand "what is my money doing in Vitana right now."

The Overview is intentionally simple — three balance cards plus a feed. The detailed views (Balance & Benefits, Subscriptions, Rewards) are sub-tabs you reach from here.

## Why it matters

Vitana has a real economy. Marketplace purchases, peer-to-peer payments, subscription billing, reward earning and staking all happen here. Without a clear snapshot, you would have to dig across screens to know where you stand. The Overview is the single page where the whole financial picture is visible in five seconds.

Wallet is also the surface that integrates with the Vitana ID for peer payments. Sending credits to `@anna` or requesting tokens from `@bob` originates on this screen.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/wallet`. Sidebar: **Wallet → Wallet Overview**. The **Wallet header button** (icon top-right of every screen) is a one-tap shortcut. There is also a global **Wallet Popup** overlay (chapter 13.7) for quick actions without leaving your current screen.

## What you see on this screen

- **Three balance cards** — Fiat, Credits, Tokens — each showing the current amount and a small trend arrow
- **Smart earnings forecast card** — projected earnings over the next 30 days from your current activity rate
- **Intelligent spending card** — suggested budget for credits and fiat based on your subscription pattern
- **Predictive actions card** — top recommended next action (e.g. "stake 100 tokens for the 30-day reward boost")
- **Dynamic reward opportunity card** — limited-time earn offers (e.g. "log 5 days this week for +50 credits")
- **Quick actions row** (mobile prominent) — Buy Credits, Add Funds, Withdraw, Buy Tokens, Stake, Send, Request
- **Recent transactions list** — last 10 transactions with type, amount, counterparty, and timestamp
- **Wallet motivational banner** — appears occasionally with engagement nudges (e.g. "You earned 200 credits this week!")
- **Sub-navigation** for Balance & Benefits, Subscriptions, Rewards

## How to use it

1. Open Wallet. The three balance cards at the top are the immediate snapshot.
2. To add fiat: tap **Add Funds**. Choose payment method, enter amount, confirm.
3. To buy credits with fiat: tap **Buy Credits** (will pull from fiat balance).
4. To send to another user: tap **Send**, enter `@handle`, choose currency and amount, confirm.
5. To request from another user: tap **Request**, enter `@handle`, amount; they get a notification.
6. To stake tokens: tap **Stake**, choose duration; staked tokens are locked for the period in exchange for a higher reward rate.
7. For the full balance breakdown: tap into **Balance & Benefits** sub-tab. For active subscriptions: **Subscriptions**. For the rewards ledger: **Rewards**.
