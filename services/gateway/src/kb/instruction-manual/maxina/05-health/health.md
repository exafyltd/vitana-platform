## What it is

The **Health Overview** screen is part of the Health module — where your Vitana Index, your pillars, your biomarkers, and your active plans live. In the navigation it sits at Health → Health Overview, and Direct URL: `/health`. This chapter explains what the screen contains, why a Maxina community user would open it, what they will see when they do, and how to act on what is there.

## Why it matters

This is the substance of what Vitana measures and improves; everything on the Autopilot ranks against the data on these screens. Every Maxina member arrives at this screen at some point in their first 30 days — sometimes via the Did You Know guided tour, sometimes by tapping a card on Home, sometimes by asking ORB "show me the health overview". Knowing why the screen exists is what stops it from feeling like noise the next time you land here.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/health`. It lives under the **Health** module of the sidebar, on the tab labelled **Health Overview**. If you ask ORB "open the health overview" the Navigator will route you straight here.

## How to use it

1. Open the screen via the sidebar (Health → Health Overview) or by asking ORB "open health overview".
2. Open Health when you want to dig past the headline number — the bars, the recent activity, the trajectory all live here.
3. If you are not sure what something on the screen means, ask ORB "what is this card?" — Vitana will read the relevant chapter section aloud.
4. To leave the screen, use the back button or open another sidebar item; nothing on this screen requires you to "save and exit" — your state is persisted automatically.
5. Many screens in the Health module pair with a related screen: see the related-screens list below for the next logical place to look.
