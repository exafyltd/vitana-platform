## What it is

Health Overview is the wellness-focused dashboard for your account — the screen where the Vitana Index, the Five Pillars, your active plans, and your most recent biology data are surfaced in a single view. Everything you need to read your day-to-day health state is here. Sub-screens go deeper (My Biology for biomarkers, Pillars for the five-pillar drill-down, Plans for ongoing programmes), but the Overview is the anchor.

The screen is also where the Vitana Index Detail can be opened with a tap, and where the Health Coach chat (a conversational health assistant) lives as an always-available card.

## Why it matters

Health is one of the busiest screens in Vitana — a high percentage of community users open it daily. Treating it well means surfacing the **delta** since yesterday (what changed, what moved your score, what needs attention) rather than just static numbers. The Overview's job is to give you that delta in five seconds.

For Maxina users on the maxina-tenant, this screen is also where curated wellness coaches and lab-order partners are surfaced — they appear as cards when relevant to your pillar gaps.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/health`. Sidebar: **Health → Health Overview**. Both the Vitana Index chip in the Home header and the AI tab's Health insights card link directly here.

## What you see on this screen

- **Header** "Health" with quick-action buttons (Health Master, Health Coach)
- **Vitana Index Mini card** — current score with trend arrow, balance factor, and a "View Detail" CTA
- **Vitana Index Trajectory card** — short-window (7 / 30 day) chart
- **Compact Vitana Index** widget — pillar bar summary
- **Motivational data card** — streak / milestone celebration when applicable
- **Next best action card** — AI-suggested single-action that would move your score most
- **Health Coach chat** — always-available conversational health assistant
- **Autopilot widget** — current wellness automation status
- **My Health Tracker** — daily nutrition / sleep / exercise log entry shortcuts
- **Mobile Health Snapshot** (mobile only) — condensed health overview card
- **Mobile Priority Focus** (mobile only) — today's wellness focus
- **Mobile Autopilot Guidance** (mobile only) — smart suggestions
- **Sub-navigation** for Services Hub, My Biology, Plans, Education, Pillars, Conditions

## How to use it

1. Open Health each morning to read the delta since yesterday.
2. Tap the Vitana Index Mini card to jump to Vitana Index Detail.
3. Tap the Next best action card to act on the single highest-impact suggestion.
4. Use Health Coach chat for ad-hoc health questions; it grounds answers in your biomarkers and pillars.
5. To log a meal / water / walk: tap the relevant My Health Tracker shortcut, or just open the Diary.
6. To go deeper: My Biology (5.3) for biomarkers, Pillars (5.6) for pillar-by-pillar drill-down, Plans (5.4) for ongoing programmes.
