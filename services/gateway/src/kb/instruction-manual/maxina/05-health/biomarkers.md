## What it is

My Biology is the screen where your real biomarker data lives — the bridge between the day-to-day pillars (Nutrition, Hydration, Exercise, Sleep, Mental) and the underlying biological signals from blood work, sleep trackers, and connected devices. Lab panels you have uploaded or ordered, ranges with green/amber/red status, and the historical trajectory of each biomarker over time all live here.

The screen distinguishes between **measured** biomarkers (real lab values you have on file) and **inferred** biomarkers (estimates Vitana builds from tracker data and your activity). Inferred values are clearly marked so you never mistake them for clinical results.

## Why it matters

The Vitana Index summarises your wellness in a single number, but the Index is downstream of the biomarkers. When something feels off — energy crashes, poor sleep, slow recovery — the Biomarker screen is where you go to look at the underlying data. It is also the screen Maxina coaches will reference if you book a consult; bringing measurable biomarkers to the conversation is the difference between a generic chat and a targeted plan.

This screen is also where you upload new lab panels (PDF or CSV) and order new tests through the integrated lab partner.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/health/biomarkers`. Sidebar: **Health → My Biology**. The screen is also linked from the Vitana Index Detail (`/health/vitana-index`) when a pillar is being held back by a specific biomarker.

## What you see on this screen

- **Header** "My Biology" with an "Upload report" button and a "Quick lab order" button
- **Summary card** — count of biomarkers in green / amber / red ranges, plus the date of your most recent panel
- **Biomarker grid** — each biomarker as a card showing the latest value, the reference range, the date measured, and a small trend sparkline
- **Filter** — narrow by panel (Lipid panel, Hormone panel, Inflammation, Vitamins, etc.) or by status (in range / borderline / out of range)
- **Trajectory chart** — historical view of any single biomarker; tap a card to expand
- **Inferred markers section** — Vitana's estimates from tracker data, clearly labelled as estimates not measurements
- **Health report upload sheet** — opens when you tap Upload report; accepts PDF and CSV
- **Quick lab order sheet** — opens the integrated lab partner flow for ordering a new panel

## How to use it

1. Open the screen and read the summary card first — it tells you how many biomarkers are out of range.
2. Tap any out-of-range card to see the historical trajectory and Vitana's "what could be driving this" hypothesis.
3. To upload a new lab panel: tap **Upload report** → choose the PDF or CSV from your device → confirm. Vitana parses it and adds the new values within a minute.
4. To order new tests: tap **Quick lab order** → choose a panel → confirm payment via the Wallet flow. Results land on this screen when the lab returns them.
5. To filter to a specific panel (e.g. Hormones): tap the panel chip at the top; the grid narrows to just those markers.
6. Discuss any out-of-range biomarker with a Maxina coach via the Discover → Doctors & Coaches screen.
