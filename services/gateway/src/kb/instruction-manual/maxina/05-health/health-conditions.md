## What it is

The **Conditions & Risks** screen is part of the Health module — where your Vitana Index, your pillars, your biomarkers, and your active plans live. In the navigation it sits at Health → Conditions & Risks, and Direct URL: `/health/conditions`. This chapter explains what the screen contains, why a Maxina community user would open it, what they will see when they do, and how to act on what is there.

## Why it matters

This is the substance of what Vitana measures and improves; everything on the Autopilot ranks against the data on these screens. Every Maxina member arrives at this screen at some point in their first 30 days — sometimes via the Did You Know guided tour, sometimes by tapping a card on Home, sometimes by asking ORB "show me the conditions & risks". Knowing why the screen exists is what stops it from feeling like noise the next time you land here.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/health/conditions`. It lives under the **Health** module of the sidebar, on the tab labelled **Conditions & Risks**. If you ask ORB "open the conditions & risks" the Navigator will route you straight here.

## How to use it

1. Open the screen via the sidebar (Health → Conditions & Risks) or by asking ORB "open conditions & risks".
2. Open Health when you want to dig past the headline number — the bars, the recent activity, the trajectory all live here.
3. If you are not sure what something on the screen means, ask ORB "what is this card?" — Vitana will read the relevant chapter section aloud.
4. To leave the screen, use the back button or open another sidebar item; nothing on this screen requires you to "save and exit" — your state is persisted automatically.
5. Many screens in the Health module pair with a related screen: see the related-screens list below for the next logical place to look.
