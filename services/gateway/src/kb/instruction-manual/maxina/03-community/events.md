## What it is

Events & Meetups is the community calendar for gatherings, workshops, online sessions, talks, challenges, and real-world meetups.

Community builds belonging, accountability, events, media, groups, content, and business opportunity through real people.

## Why it matters

Events turn digital community into lived connection. They support mental health, learning, movement, relationships, and business networking.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/community/events`.

Sidebar path: **Community → Events & Meetups**.

Module: **Community**.

## What you see on this screen

- Event list or calendar with date, host, place, capacity, price, and reward details
- Filters for date, location, topic, online, and in-person
- RSVP, ticket, save, share, and Add to Calendar actions
- Meetup and Event Detail drawers

## How to use it

1. Filter by time, location, or goal.
2. Open the event drawer to review host, agenda, and requirements.
3. RSVP or buy a ticket only after confirmation.
4. Add the event to Calendar and set a reminder.
5. Use attendee context for matching when appropriate.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Find events and meetups."
- "What is happening this week?"
- "RSVP to this event."
- "Add this to my calendar."
