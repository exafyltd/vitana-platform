## What it is

My Business is the community entry point for building income inside Maxina through services, clients, offers, events, referrals, content, and partnerships.

Community builds belonging, accountability, events, media, groups, content, and business opportunity through real people.

## Why it matters

Vitana supports economic independence as part of quality of life. This screen helps the user move from profile to offer, from offer to audience, and from audience to income.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/community/my-business`.

Sidebar path: **Community → My Business**.

Module: **Community**.

## What you see on this screen

- Business readiness, services, offers, and lead cards
- Client, partner, or match suggestions
- Revenue, reward, or commission indicators
- Links to Profile, Campaigns, Wallet, Marketplace, and Find a Match

## How to use it

1. Check profile completeness first.
2. Define one clear offer or service.
3. Use Find a Match to locate clients, partners, or collaborators.
4. Promote through Campaigns, Feed, Groups, or Events.
5. Track value in Wallet and Rewards.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Help me build a business."
- "Create my first service."
- "Find clients for me."
- "Show my business earnings."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

Community builds belonging, accountability, events, media, groups, content, and business opportunity through real people. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
