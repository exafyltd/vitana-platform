## What it is

Media Hub is the community media center for shorts, videos, images, music, and podcasts. Users can watch, listen, upload, comment, and save media.

Community builds belonging, accountability, events, media, groups, content, and business opportunity through real people.

## Why it matters

Media lets creators teach, inspire, and build trust. It also gives users a richer way to participate than text alone and can become part of the longevity economy.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/community/media-hub`.

Sidebar path: **Community → Media Hub**.

Module: **Community**.

## What you see on this screen

- Shorts, videos, podcast, music, and image feeds
- Upload controls for video, image, audio, and podcast content
- Playback, comments, reactions, save, and share controls
- Creator attribution, moderation, and report actions

## How to use it

1. Choose Watch, Listen, or Upload.
2. Filter by pillar, topic, event, or creator.
3. Open media details before commenting or sharing.
4. Upload only content the user has rights to publish.
5. Save useful media to Memory or use Sharing for campaigns.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Watch shorts."
- "Upload a video."
- "Play podcasts."
- "Post this comment."
