## What it is

The **Media Hub** screen is part of the Community module — the social layer of Maxina where you meet, attend, and grow with other members. In the navigation it sits at Community → Media Hub, and Direct URL: `/community/media-hub`. This chapter explains what the screen contains, why a Maxina community user would open it, what they will see when they do, and how to act on what is there.

## Why it matters

Longevity and wellness compound when they are shared; the community surface is where motivation and accountability come from. Every Maxina member arrives at this screen at some point in their first 30 days — sometimes via the Did You Know guided tour, sometimes by tapping a card on Home, sometimes by asking ORB "show me the media hub". Knowing why the screen exists is what stops it from feeling like noise the next time you land here.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/community/media-hub`. It lives under the **Community** module of the sidebar, on the tab labelled **Media Hub**. If you ask ORB "open the media hub" the Navigator will route you straight here.

## How to use it

1. Open the screen via the sidebar (Community → Media Hub) or by asking ORB "open media hub".
2. Browse, RSVP, or join — most actions reward credits and grow your community standing.
3. If you are not sure what something on the screen means, ask ORB "what is this card?" — Vitana will read the relevant chapter section aloud.
4. To leave the screen, use the back button or open another sidebar item; nothing on this screen requires you to "save and exit" — your state is persisted automatically.
