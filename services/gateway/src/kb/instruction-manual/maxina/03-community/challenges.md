## What it is

Challenges is the Challenges screen in the Community module. It gives the user a focused place to understand the current state, choose the next step, and ask Vitana for help.

## Why it matters

Community builds belonging, accountability, events, media, groups, content, and business opportunity through real people. It should help the user live better through the five longevity pillars while making useful community or income opportunities easier to act on.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/community/challenges`.

## What you see on this screen

- People, posts, groups, events, or media cards
- Search and filters for topic, date, location, or interest
- Connect, RSVP, join, comment, upload, follow, or share actions
- Safety, moderation, and privacy controls

## How to use it

1. Choose the user intent: meet people, attend events, join groups, watch media, post, or build business.
2. Open the relevant card and read context before acting.
3. Use Profile Preview before messaging or connecting.
4. Confirm before posting publicly or sharing personal data.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Who is in the community?"
- "Find events and meetups."
- "Upload this short."
- "Post this comment."
