## What it is

Live Rooms are real-time audio and video rooms hosted by Maxina community members. Think of it as the live-conversation layer of the community — at any moment a creator, a coach, or a member may have an open room you can drop into to listen, speak, or watch. Some rooms are scheduled (announced in advance); others are spontaneous. Some are public to all Maxina members; others are gated to a specific group.

Each room has a host, a topic, a participant count, and a join button. When a room is in progress, you see it in the "Active now" section; when one is upcoming, it appears in the schedule.

## Why it matters

Most community on Vitana is asynchronous (posts, events, messages). Live Rooms are the synchronous layer — the difference between leaving a comment and being in the conversation. They are also where coaching sessions, Q&A with creators, group meditations, accountability check-ins, and "ask me anything" sessions happen. For Maxina specifically, Live Rooms are the most direct line between members and the community curators.

Joining a room earns reward credits and contributes to your community standing on the Member Rankings.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/community/live-rooms`. Sidebar: **Community → Live Rooms**. On mobile, Live Rooms is also one of the four bottom-navigation shortcuts so it is one tap away from any screen.

## What you see on this screen

- **Header** with "Live Rooms" title and a refresh icon
- **Active now** section — currently-live rooms, each with the host's avatar, room topic, current participant count, and a green "Live" badge
- **Coming up** section — scheduled rooms with start time, topic, and an RSVP button
- **My rooms** section — rooms you are hosting or have RSVPed to, surfaced separately for easy access
- **Filter chips** — narrow by category (Health, Mindset, Movement, Nutrition, Community)
- **Join button** on each card — tap to enter
- **Host badge** on rooms led by a verified Maxina coach or creator

## How to use it

1. Browse the Active now section — these are happening this moment. The participant count tells you which rooms have momentum.
2. Tap a room card to read the topic and host bio before joining.
3. Tap **Join** to enter. You arrive muted by default; tap the mic to request to speak (the host approves speaker requests).
4. To attend a future session: scroll to Coming up, tap **RSVP**. You'll get a notification 5 minutes before it starts; tapping the notification jumps you straight in.
5. To host your own room: tap the **+ New Room** button (Maxina members can host one room at a time; verified creators can host more).
6. Joining and participating in a room (≥10 minutes) earns reward credits, logged on Wallet → Rewards.
