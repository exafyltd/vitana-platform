## What it is

Daily Diary is the primary capture surface for everything you want Vitana to remember. It supports three modes — text, voice (dictation), and photo — and every entry lands on your Memory Timeline, gets parsed by the diary-health-extractor, and contributes to the relevant pillar of your Vitana Index. Voice is the most-used mode by design: tap the mic, speak a sentence, and Vitana parses what you said into structured entries (a glass of water, a walk, a meeting with Anna, a journal reflection on today).

Diary entries are private by default — they live in your Memory and feed your Index, but nothing is shared with the community unless you explicitly publish it.

## Why it matters

Most wellness apps require you to fill in forms. Vitana inverts that: just talk. The Diary is the friction-removal that makes daily logging possible — "I drank a glass of water" takes two seconds and lifts your Hydration pillar; "I'm feeling stuck on this project" takes five seconds and feeds your Mental pillar plus your relationship + project memory.

Diary entries also drive **streaks** (3-day, 7-day, 14-day, 30-day) which earn reward credits and unlock Autopilot capacity. The Diary is the most direct path to making Vitana feel personal — every entry is a piece of the picture.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/memory/diary`. Sidebar: **Memory → Daily Diary**. There is also a dedicated **Diary Orb** floating on the Diary screen that captures voice with a single tap, distinct from the global ORB overlay.

## What you see on this screen

- **Header** "Daily Diary" with the current streak count (e.g. "3-day streak")
- **Three capture buttons** — Text, Voice, Photo
- **Text editor** — opens when you tap Text; type freely, save with the Save Diary button
- **Voice recorder** — opens when you tap Voice; tap the mic to start, tap again or stop talking to finish
- **Photo uploader** — opens when you tap Photo; pick from camera or library, add an optional caption
- **Today's entries list** — the entries you have already logged today, with the time and a one-line preview each
- **Recent days carousel** — yesterday and the days before, scrollable left-right
- **Pillar lift indicator** — after each save, a brief toast shows which pillar(s) just lifted
- **Diary Orb** — the dedicated voice-capture sphere on this screen (separate from the global ORB)

## How to use it

1. Open Daily Diary. Read the streak count at the top — it is your current logging momentum.
2. To log by voice (recommended): tap the mic and speak. "I just had three glasses of water." "I went for a 30-minute walk after lunch." "I'm feeling anxious about tomorrow's meeting." One sentence per topic is fine; Vitana parses it.
3. To log by text: tap Text, type your entry, tap Save.
4. To log by photo: tap Photo, capture or upload, optionally add a caption explaining what is in the photo.
5. After saving, watch for the pillar-lift toast — it tells you which pillar(s) just got credit. If a pillar didn't lift when you expected it to, ORB will explain why next time you ask.
6. Aim for a **3-day streak** as your first goal — at 3 days you unlock the first Diary reward, at 7 days you unlock the next, etc.
