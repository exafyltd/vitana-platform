## What it is

The **Insights** screen is part of the AI tab — the surfaces dedicated to insights, recommendations, and the personal AI companion. In the navigation it sits at AI → Insights, and Direct URL: `/ai/insights`. This chapter explains what the screen contains, why a Maxina community user would open it, what they will see when they do, and how to act on what is there.

## Why it matters

The AI tab is where Vitana shows its work — what it inferred about you, what it would do next, and why. Every Maxina member arrives at this screen at some point in their first 30 days — sometimes via the Did You Know guided tour, sometimes by tapping a card on Home, sometimes by asking ORB "show me the insights". Knowing why the screen exists is what stops it from feeling like noise the next time you land here.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/ai/insights`. It lives under the **AI** module of the sidebar, on the tab labelled **Insights**. If you ask ORB "open the insights" the Navigator will route you straight here.

## How to use it

1. Open the screen via the sidebar (AI → Insights) or by asking ORB "open insights".
2. Open it weekly to read the Daily Summary trend; tap into Recommendations to see what the Autopilot would queue up.
3. If you are not sure what something on the screen means, ask ORB "what is this card?" — Vitana will read the relevant chapter section aloud.
4. To leave the screen, use the back button or open another sidebar item; nothing on this screen requires you to "save and exit" — your state is persisted automatically.
5. Many screens in the AI module pair with a related screen: see the related-screens list below for the next logical place to look.
