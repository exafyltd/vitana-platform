## What it is

Daily Summary is the Daily Summary screen in the AI module. It gives the user a focused place to understand the current state, choose the next step, and ask Vitana for help.

AI screens show insights, recommendations, summaries, and companion conversation so users can understand what Vitana is doing.

## Why it matters

AI screens show insights, recommendations, summaries, and companion conversation so users can understand what Vitana is doing. It should help the user live better through the five longevity pillars while making useful community or income opportunities easier to act on.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/ai/daily-summary`.

Sidebar path: **AI → Daily Summary**.

Module: **AI**.

## What you see on this screen

- Insight, recommendation, summary, or conversation cards
- Reason, source, confidence, and freshness labels
- Approve, dismiss, correct, save, or ask-follow-up actions
- Autopilot and Memory links

## How to use it

1. Read the reason before accepting a suggestion.
2. Ask why when the inference is unclear.
3. Correct or dismiss wrong outputs.
4. Confirm before Vitana sends, schedules, buys, shares, or changes anything.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "What did you notice today?"
- "Explain this recommendation."
- "Run these actions."
- "Talk with me."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

AI screens show insights, recommendations, summaries, and companion conversation so users can understand what Vitana is doing. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
