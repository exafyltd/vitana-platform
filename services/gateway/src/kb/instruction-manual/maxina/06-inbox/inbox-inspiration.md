## What it is

Inspiration is the Inspiration screen in the Inbox module. It gives the user a focused place to understand the current state, choose the next step, and ask Vitana for help.

Inbox keeps reminders, inspiration, notifications, and follow-ups from getting lost.

## Why it matters

Inbox keeps reminders, inspiration, notifications, and follow-ups from getting lost. It should help the user live better through the five longevity pillars while making useful community or income opportunities easier to act on.

## Where to find it

Direct URL: `/inbox/inspiration`.

Sidebar path: **Inbox → Inspiration**.

Module: **Inbox**.

## What you see on this screen

- Active, due, archived, or inspirational items
- Source labels and timestamps
- Complete, snooze, restore, archive, or delete controls
- Links back to Calendar, Diary, Events, Plans, or Support

## How to use it

1. Separate urgent reminders from softer inspiration.
2. Complete tasks that can be done now.
3. Snooze useful items that are badly timed.
4. Archive or delete stale items after confirmation.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "What reminders are due?"
- "Snooze this."
- "Restore this item."
- "Inspire me."

## Guidance for Vitana Assistant

Inbox keeps reminders, inspiration, notifications, and follow-ups from getting lost. Explain the screen in plain language first, then offer the next safe action. Ask for confirmation before sending messages, making purchases, booking services, changing privacy, sharing data, altering subscriptions, or deleting anything.
