## What it is

Find a Match brings the right people together. A match can be a friend, accountability partner, event companion, coach, client, creator, business partner, or collaborator. Vitana uses profile context, interests, Life Compass, community activity, location, events, content, and business intent to explain why a person is relevant.

## Why it matters

Many health and income breakthroughs are relational. The right person can help the user stay consistent, attend an event, learn a skill, start a service, or build a new business. Matching is therefore both a wellbeing feature and an economic feature.

## Where to find it

Use Home -> Matches for daily matches. Use Community, Groups, Events, Media Hub, Profile Preview, and My Business for context-specific matching.

## How to use it

1. Ask what kind of match the user wants: health buddy, friend, event attendee, coach, client, partner, or business collaborator.
2. Check profile completeness because weak profiles produce weaker matches.
3. Use filters such as interest, location, event, group, goal, or business intent.
4. Open Profile Preview before recommending contact.
5. Draft a message and ask for confirmation before sending it.
6. Save match feedback so future matches improve.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Find me a match."
- "Who should I meet in the community?"
- "Find a client for my service."
- "Find a partner for a new business."
- "Send a message to this match."
