## What it is

Marketplace Autopilot is the set of assistant-supported workflows that help users find, operate, and grow marketplace activity with minimal manual effort. It can suggest products, providers, services, campaigns, referrals, matches, and next business actions. It should always explain why an opportunity is relevant and what permission is required.

## Why it matters

A marketplace becomes useful when it saves time and creates value. Autopilot can help match demand with supply, remind users to follow up, draft campaign messages, surface unserved needs, and connect offers to the right people. This supports the mission of health improvement and sustainable income.

## Where to find it

Marketplace Autopilot appears through Discover, My Business, Sharing, Campaigns, Wallet Rewards, Recommendations, and Data & Consent. Some actions may surface as Autopilot cards rather than a standalone screen.

## How to use it

1. Define the goal: buy, book, sell, promote, refer, or partner.
2. Let Vitana search for relevant products, services, providers, clients, or offers.
3. Review the reason and privacy implications for each recommendation.
4. Approve drafts, messages, bookings, purchases, or campaigns before execution.
5. Track outcomes in Wallet, Campaigns, Orders, and My Business.
6. Give feedback on poor matches or irrelevant offers.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Let Autopilot find an opportunity."
- "Promote my service."
- "Find marketplace gaps."
- "Draft a referral message."
- "Show what earned money for me."
