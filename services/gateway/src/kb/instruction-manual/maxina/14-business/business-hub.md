## What it is

Business Hub is the instruction layer for users who want to create income inside the Maxina community. Depending on the current app surface, the user may access it through My Business, Marketplace, Wallet, Sharing, Campaigns, Profile Edit, or Find a Match. The purpose is to help the user turn skills, services, content, events, referrals, or partnerships into a sustainable opportunity.

## Why it matters

Vitana's mission includes economic independence. A person who improves their health and builds meaningful income has more freedom, resilience, and quality of life. Business setup should therefore be treated as part of the longevity journey, not as a separate commercial add-on.

## Where to find it

Use Community -> My Business as the current user-facing entry point. Related flows live in Profile Edit, Discover, Sharing, Campaigns, Wallet Rewards, and Data & Consent.

## How to use it

1. Start by enriching the profile with skills, interests, location, languages, and services.
2. Define one clear offer: coaching, class, consulting, product, event, content, or partnership.
3. Use Find a Match to locate possible clients, partners, or collaborators.
4. Publish the offer through Marketplace or Sharing when available.
5. Track rewards, commissions, subscriptions, and payouts in Wallet.
6. Let Autopilot suggest the next business action, but confirm public or financial steps.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Help me build a business."
- "What service could I offer?"
- "Find people who need my skill."
- "Create a campaign for my offer."
- "Show my business earnings."
