## What it is

Services, Clients, and Delivery covers the practical operating loop after a user decides to earn income. The user defines an offer, publishes or shares it, receives interest, schedules delivery, serves the client, and tracks reward or payment state.

## Why it matters

Income is not created by visibility alone. A useful business flow needs clear offers, trusted profiles, reliable scheduling, good follow-up, and transparent wallet outcomes. Vitana should help reduce the operational load so a small provider can act professionally.

## Where to find it

Use My Business for the business surface, Calendar for delivery timing, Profile Edit and Public Profile for trust, Campaigns for promotion, Wallet for rewards and payments, and Support for disputes.

## How to use it

1. Create one specific service with outcome, duration, price, and audience.
2. Make sure the public profile supports trust for that service.
3. Promote through Campaigns, Feed, Groups, Events, or Find a Match.
4. Schedule accepted work on Calendar.
5. Use reminders for preparation and follow-up.
6. Track earnings and refunds in Wallet and Orders.

## What you can ask Vitana

- "Create a service offer."
- "Find clients for this service."
- "Schedule a client session."
- "Send a follow-up message."
- "Show revenue from this offer."
