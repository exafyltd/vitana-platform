# Subscriptions

Your subscription is how you choose the level of support you want on your longevity journey. Vitanaland offers different plans because different people are at different stages of their health path, and what you need today might be different from what you need six months from now. Let me explain what is available, what each plan offers, and how to make the choice that is right for you.

## Free vs. Premium

Every member of Vitanaland starts with access to the core platform at no cost. This is intentional. We believe that the fundamentals of longevity guidance should be accessible to everyone, not locked behind a paywall. Your basic access includes:

- **Health pillar tracking.** You can log your nutrition, hydration, exercise, sleep, and mental health from day one.
- **Your ORB companion.** I am here for every member. You can talk to me, ask questions, get guidance, and build your relationship with me regardless of your subscription level.
- **Community access.** You can join communities, participate in discussions, attend public events, and build your social connections.
- **Your Vitana Index.** Your vitality score is calculated and available to you on every plan.
- **Memory Garden.** Your personal memory space is always active, storing what you share with me so I can personalize your experience.
- **Credit earning.** You earn credits through engagement and healthy behaviors on every plan.

This baseline is substantial. You can have a meaningful longevity experience inside Vitanaland without ever paying for a subscription. Many members do exactly that, and their health trajectories are strong.

### What Premium Unlocks

Premium subscriptions go deeper. They are designed for members who want more precision, more access, and more personalized support. Here is what premium plans add:

**Advanced health analytics.** Premium unlocks deeper insights into your health data. You get more detailed breakdowns of your pillar performance, trend analysis over longer time periods, and predictive insights that help you anticipate where your health trajectory is heading -- not just where it has been.

**Enhanced personalization.** While every member receives personalized guidance from me, premium members receive more granular personalization. I draw on a wider set of data signals, offer more specific recommendations, and provide more detailed context for every suggestion I make. The difference is depth, not quality -- I always give my best guidance, but premium lets me go further.

**Priority access to professionals.** Premium members get priority booking with wellness practitioners, health coaches, and specialists within the marketplace. If a popular practitioner has limited availability, premium members see those openings first.

**Exclusive content and programs.** Certain educational content, specialized programs, and expert-led courses are available only to premium members. These are not generic wellness guides. They are deep, evidence-based resources created by longevity professionals.

**Earn while you participate.** Your healthy behaviors and your community contributions generate reward credits as you go. Those credits unlock community extras like additional match reveals or extra photo uploads in the Memory Garden. Earned rewards stay earned -- they accumulate without expiring as long as you remain active.

**Premium community features.** Access to premium-only live events, intimate group sessions with limited attendance, and exclusive community spaces designed for deeper engagement and more focused discussion.

## Our four promises

Before plans, before pricing, here is what stays true on every plan -- free or paid:

1. **You own what you share.** Your memory, your profile, your recommendations are visible to you, exportable on request, and deletable at any time.
2. **We never sell your data to advertisers, brokers, or partners.** We never have. The system enforces this in code.
3. **Your experience and your recommendations can earn for you.** When your trusted recommendation closes a sale through Sell and Earn, or when you host a paid Live Room, you receive the income directly. Every cent is shown to you.
4. **You decide what Sell and Earn does for you.** Three switches in your Connect settings give you full control over whether your assistant receives, gives, or earns from recommendations -- and which channels it listens to.

The Trust Center in your settings shows exactly what stays on your device, what lives on Vitana servers, and what is sent to the AI provider when you talk to me. No hidden profiling. No surprises.

## Available Plans

Most members choose between Free and Premium. Two larger plans exist for hosts, coaches, and community leaders who use Live Rooms heavily or build their work around the platform.

**Free.** Full access to core features. No cost, no commitment, no expiration. This is your permanent foundation inside Vitanaland.

**Premium.** Daily wellness use. Live conversations with me, room hosting time, and storage scaled for an engaged member. **€9.99 per month**, or **€89 per year** -- the annual plan saves about a quarter of the monthly cost because we want to reward long-term commitment, just like longevity rewards consistency.

**Host.** Five times the Premium quotas, designed for power users, families, and anyone hosting regular Live Rooms or working with multiple recommendations. **€99 per month** or **€890 per year**.

**Community.** Twenty times the Premium quotas, designed for coaches, practitioners, and community leads who run their work through the platform. **€199 per month** or **€1990 per year**.

Every paid plan includes a **14-day free trial** on first subscription. You can cancel anytime during the trial without being charged.

## How to Subscribe

Subscribing is simple and happens entirely within your wallet:

1. Open your wallet and navigate to the Subscriptions section.
2. Review the available plans and what each one includes.
3. Select the plan that fits your needs.
4. Choose your payment method -- you can use a saved payment method or add a new one.
5. Confirm your subscription.

Your premium features activate immediately. There is no waiting period. From the moment you subscribe, every premium benefit is available to you.

## How to Upgrade or Change Plans

Your subscription is flexible. If you start with the monthly plan and decide to switch to annual for the discount, you can do that from your wallet at any time. The system prorates your existing subscription and applies the difference to your new plan.

If you are on the free tier and ready to go premium, upgrading takes just a few taps. And if you are on an annual plan but want to return to monthly, you can make that change at the end of your current billing cycle.

I always confirm changes before they take effect, so you are never surprised by an unexpected charge or plan modification.

## How to Cancel

Cancellation is straightforward and does not require contacting support or navigating a maze of retention screens. If you decide to cancel your premium subscription:

1. Go to the Subscriptions section in your wallet.
2. Select your active subscription.
3. Choose Cancel.
4. Confirm.

Your premium access remains active through the end of your current billing period. You have already paid for that time, and you should receive full value from it. After the billing period ends, your account returns to the free tier with all core features intact.

Cancelling does not delete your account, your data, your Memory Garden, or your credit balance. Everything you have built inside Vitanaland stays with you. If you decide to resubscribe later, you pick up right where you left off.

I do not want you to feel trapped by a subscription. If premium is not delivering value for you right now, cancelling is the right decision. Your longevity journey does not stop when your subscription does -- you still have access to everything the free tier offers, and I am still here for you.

## Billing and Receipts

Every subscription payment is recorded in your transaction history. You can review your billing dates, payment amounts, and receipt details at any time from within your wallet.

**Billing reminders.** I notify you before each billing cycle so you are never caught off guard. You always know when your next payment is coming and how much it will be.

**Payment failures.** If a payment fails -- an expired card, insufficient funds, or a processing issue -- I let you know immediately and give you time to update your payment method before your access is affected. There is a grace period specifically so that a temporary payment issue does not interrupt your premium experience.

**Receipts.** Full receipts for every subscription payment are available in your transaction history. They include all the details you might need for personal record-keeping.

## What Premium Means for Your Longevity

I want to be honest with you about what premium does and does not do.

Premium does not make me care about you more. I care about every member of this community equally. Premium does not unlock some secret health algorithm that free members miss out on. The core longevity intelligence is the same for everyone.

What premium does is give you more tools, more depth, and more access. It is like the difference between a standard gym membership and one that includes personal training sessions. The gym is the same. The equipment is the same. But the personal attention, the specialized programming, and the priority access to experts can accelerate your progress significantly.

If you are at a stage in your journey where that acceleration matters -- where deeper analytics, more personalized guidance, and priority access to professionals would make a real difference -- premium is worth considering. If you are happy with the core experience and making strong progress on the free tier, that is perfectly valid too.

The longevity economy is designed so that your subscription, whatever level it is at, supports a journey that gets stronger over time. The most important investment is showing up every day. Everything else -- including your subscription level -- is a choice you make based on what serves you best right now.
